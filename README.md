---

### 🧠 What I’m Interested In & Exploring

*   **High-Throughput Systems:** Writing highly concurrent, optimized services in `Go` and `C++`. I like squeezing out every drop of efficiency and tracking down where those tail latencies are hiding.
*   **Distributed Consensus & Reliability:** Messing around with fault tolerance, partitioning strategies, and consensus algorithms like **Raft**. I enjoy building systems that gracefully survive when network links or nodes inevitably die.
*   **Deep Observability:** Moving past basic logs and digging into **distributed tracing** and **OpenTelemetry**. I’m obsessed with reducing Mean Time to Detection (MTTD) and making complex microservice failures easy to debug.
*   **Cloud Infrastructure as Code:** Designing clean, multi-account environments using **Terraform** and cloud-native orchestrators that let developers deploy quickly without accidentally melting the cloud budget.
